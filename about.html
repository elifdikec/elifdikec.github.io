About

Elif Dikeç is an Istanbul-based pianist, producer, and composer. She holds degrees in Classical Piano Performance (BM) from Yıldız Technical University and in Popular Music (MM) from the Institut für Populäre Musik at Folkwang Universität der Künste.

She has composed and performed for various musical projects in both Turkey and Germany, collaborating with artists as well as prestigious theatres such as the Düsseldorfer Schauspielhaus.

She is currently the touring keyboardist and musical director (MD) for the live band of renowned Turkish singer-songwriter Melike Şahin.

Dikeç continues her versatile work in production, arrangement, collaboration, and session musicianship between Istanbul and Cologne.
